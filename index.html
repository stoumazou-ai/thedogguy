Meet the Dog Guy

Hi, I'm Antonis.

Animals and nature have been the great loves of my life for as long as I can remember. That passion took me to various parts of the world, exploring the extraordinary creatures we share this planet with — and everywhere I went, it was the animals I connected with first.

Three years ago, I turned that lifelong love into my craft. Dog grooming lets me do what I'm happiest doing: spending my days surrounded by these amazing creatures, earning their trust, and caring for them the way they deserve.

I've always had a way with dogs — calm hands, a patient manner, and a genuine bond that nervous and first-time pups pick up on straight away. When your dog leaves looking and feeling their best, that's the best part of my day.

— Antonis
